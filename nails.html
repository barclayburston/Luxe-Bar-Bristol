Hands and Toes

Give your hands or feet a LUXE treat and select from our extensive range of hands or toe treatments, from a luxury manicure or Luxury pedicure. Working with leading innovative brands, product and the latest techniques, we offer a range of nail art, nail foils and a full range of Gel colours including the gel bottle builder gel.

The GelBottle Inc™ gel polishes are rich, highly pigmented, chip-resistant and offer extremely long lasting shine. The good news doesn’t end there because they are also vegan and cruelty-free!

Builder In A Bottle™, aka BIAB, are a range of soak off gel builders ideal for creating strong overlays and as a strengthening barrier for natural nails.
No chipping or lifting, which helps grow natural nails

Manicure
Give your hands and nails the care they deserve with our manicure using Kaeso products.

Your hands will be transformed with our deep exfoliation and revitalising massage, together with our cuticle work and shaping all ready for your chosen Gel colour or treatment to finish.

Nourishing mask treatments or heated mitts can be added to our manicure for extra moisturisation in a luxury.

Pedicure
Sit back and relax as your feet are refreshed and revitalised. Our deep exfoliating scrub will eliminate tired skin cells and hard skin will be removed followed by a deep leg and foot massage.

Cuticle work and filing to your desired length is carried out all ready for your chosen Gel colour. Deep conditioning mask can be added to your pedicure for extra moisturisation. A callus peel treatment is also offered for cracked, dry, or tough skin and problem heels.

Also Builder Gel can be added to manicure or pedicure treatment at a small additional cost, giving you long lasting, chip free nails!
