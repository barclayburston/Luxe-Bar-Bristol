BROWS

It’s a well-known fact that eyebrows can frame your face and accentuate your features which is why, here at LUXE Bar we offer several high impact brow services including HD Brows.

Eyebrow Wax and Tint

Frame your face with our perfect brows which consists of a shape using wax and a tint to enhance your new shape. When your shape has been achieved it can be maintained with visits to the salon, usually monthly depending on the speed of your hair growth.

HD Brows

High definition brows is a bespoke brow treatment, tailored exclusively for you. Assessing face shape and colouring, it includes tinting waxing and threading focusing on the design of the brows.

Even if you have sparse areas we can put you on a regrowth programme. We also show you at the end of each treatment how to fill in these sparse areas using High definition brow products which can be purchased after your treatment if you wish.

Each treatment lasts up to 30-45 minutes.
