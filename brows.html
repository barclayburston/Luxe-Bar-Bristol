HD Brows

High definition brows is a bespoke brow treatment, tailored exclusively for you. Assessing face shape and colouring, it includes tinting waxing and threading focusing on the design of the brows. Each treatment lasts up to 30-45 minutes. Even if you have sparse areas we can put you on a regrowth programme. We also show you at the end of each treatment how to fill in these sparse areas using High definition brow products which can be purchased after your treatment if you wish.

Please note that if you are having any brow tinting then you will need a skin test 24 hours prior to your treatment. Simply pop in without an appointment to get yours done.
