Hello and huge warm welcome to Luxe Bar!

We are an independent beauty salon based within a Style Creation hair salon in Hanham High Street, only 15 minutes from Bristol city centre.

With 13 years in the beauty industry and with 10 years experience of eyelash treatments you can expect 5* service when you visit. The driving force behind Luxe Bar is to help women feel beautiful, promoting great skincare and loving the body you are in. From eyelashes to waxing, there is nothing in your way to stop you feeling your best beautiful!
