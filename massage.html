Massage

Here at LUXE Bar our experienced masseurs offer different massage techniques to adapt to your needs, from Swedish and deep tissue massages to holistic approaches with relaxation and essential oils being the key focus. Our experienced therapists will help you understand what best will suit you and offer a personalised treatment plan for you. As you arrive into our tranquil and calm treatment room the warm lighting and gentle music welcomes and calms you. You will leave the salon feeling refreshed, relaxed and revitalised. Our massage is designed to increase blood flow and relieve your everyday aches and pains.

Our Back, Neck and Shoulder Massages are designed to relieve the stress that we tend to carry on our shoulders, with a mixture of essential oils and deep tissue work if required, this will leave you feeling refreshed and relaxed.

The Full Body Massages offered from LUXE Bar is customised to each individual. Typically essential oils as an aromatherapy treatment, or holistic approaches tend to heal and balance your body. Your masseuse will communicate with you to heal problem areas, and are experienced in using deep tissue massage techniques when required to get rid of those tense areas.

Pregnancy Massage

A unique and popular treatment for mums to be who are restless and fatigued. With specific mother and baby oils, this massage is offered to mums that are past the first 12 weeks of pregnancy for some much needed R&R.

Deep Tissue Massage

Perfect for stubborn underlying problems and for those that have gone without massage for some time. We break down problem areas using slower movements combined with deep stretches to ease pain and bring back ease of movement.

Warm Bamboo Massage/ Hot Stone Massage

Our team are experienced in warm bamboo massages and hot stone massages. A technique that increases relaxation and a sense of calm, this extension of a normal massage soothes aches and pains, detoxes and heals and gives the mind a sense of elevated wellness. Using the highest quality of Luxury warm bamboo and Hot Stone, the treatments are known to improve circulation, increase energy levels, release stored tension, relieve symptoms of anxiety and depression, and help insomnia sufferers.

Your therapist is on hand every step of the way to maximise your comfort as the warm and oiled bamboo or stones relieve pressure as you drift off into a relaxed state.

Indian Head Massage

LUXE Bar offers a take on the ancient Indian Head massage treatment, by use of the traditional system of medicine ‘the science of life and longevity.’ It is based around the balance within the body, mind and spirit, and is said to promote a long life. Indian Head massages originally only focused on the head area, but are now extended to focus on the upper arms, neck and shoulders too.

Our Indian Head Massage has a variety of health benefits including increasing blood circulation, increasing lymphatic flow to the head which eliminates toxins and waste products, and stimulates the release of endorphins from the brain, relieving notions of anxiety, depression and emotional stress. The massage leaves you with a sense of clearer thinking and concentration and can benefit everyone with the treatment adaptable around your needs.
