things to know

patch tests

We require patch tests to be carried out up to 24 hours before some of our treatments that involve tint or products near the eye. Patch testing is required for eyebrow/eyelash tint, HD brows, eyelash extensions and Lash Lifts.


tanning

We highly recommend clients do a full body exfoliation 24 hours before their tanning appointment. On the day please avoid using any oily products and wear loose clothing when coming for your treatment.


tanning aftercare

Do not get wet for the next 8-12 hours
Do not touch your skin, colour can transfer to your palms and can be difficult to remove
The first shower should be a rinse only, wait until the shower water runs clear
Use gentle body washes, bar soap can strip the skin therefore the tan as well
Moisturise the skin two times a day for longer lasting tan
USE SPF, a spray tan will NOT protect you from the sun
Around day 5, begin to exfoliate again, this will help with a even fading of colour
Completely exfoliate the tan off before getting your next spray tan


pedicure

For our amazing pedicures we recommend bringing some open toe shoes or flip flops with you to your appointment. After your appointment you can relax in our waiting area with a refreshment while your toes dry.


eyelash extensions

Please arrive to your appointment without any eye make up if our lash artist has to remove your make up it may cut into your treatment time. We do not complete eyelash infills from other's work, only on eyelash extensions that have been completed by LUXE Bar.


eyelash extensions aftercare

Avoid getting your lashes wet for at least 24 hours after your treatment
Avoid the use of a sauna, steam room, swimming pool and sunbeds until 48 hours after your treatment
Avoid going to the gym, hot yoga or any activities that will make you sweat for at least 24 hours after the treatment
Avoid touching your lashes
Do not wear mascara on the eyelash extensions
Avoid any oily products around your eyelashes (Oil based shampoo, conditioners, creams, creamy eye shadows, mascara)
Eyeliner can be a devil with eyelash extensions
Wash/Wipe your lashes daily after 24 hours, your eyelash artist will assist you and show you how to do this safely
Pulling, picking and rubbing your eyes will effect the retention of your lashes and could cause damage
Book your infill and enjoy your new lush lashes!


waxing aftercare

For the next 24-48 hours:
No hot baths or showers (cool lukewarm water only)
No saunas, hot tubs, massage or steam treatments
No tanning (sunbathing, sunbeds or self tan)
No excessive exercise
Avoid scratching or touching the treated area with unwashed hands
Avoid swimming in chlorinated pools
Do not apply deodorants, body sprays, powders, lotions or other products to that area other then those recommended by your therapist.
And lastly, Enjoy your smooth skin!


massage and facial treatments aftercare

Your dedicated therapist will tailor make your treatments to suit your need, they will deliver tailor made aftercare for you at the end of your treatment. Drinking plenty of water is always a great way to flush toxins after a massage or facial treatment. Annnnnnd relax!

bookings, cancellations and changes

The following terms apply to all appointments. At least 24 hours notice is requested if you need to cancel or change your appointment; otherwise the late cancellation costs as laid out below will be charged. Cancellation or change of treatment times within 24 hours of your booking will incur a charge of 100% of the full treatment value or full payment for treatments if under £10. As we are unable to judge reasons for change, cancellation or non-arrival, clients are reminded that no exceptions to the above conditions will be made and that any booking made with the salon (verbal or written) forms a legally binding contract regarding this.

late arrivals

Please note that if clients arrive late for an appointment the treatment time will be shortened to ensure subsequent treatments run on time. The full price of the treatment will be charged.


children

We respectfully ask that adults do not bring children under the age of 14 into the salon for health and safety reasons and to keep noise levels to a minimum.


confidentiality

We take the issue of client confidentiality very seriously. All personal information and treatment details are kept securely and in confidence and will not be disclosed to any third party outside of the Luxe Bar business.


gift vouchers

Luxe Bar vouchers are available all year round and the make the perfect gift for someone special, Birthdays, Anniversaries, Christmas or an ideal way to say “Thank You”. Our gift vouchers let the recipient choose their perfect beauty treatment or combination of beauty treatments, which they can redeem whenever they choose and do not have to be redeemed in one transaction making them completely flexible. Our gift vouchers have a 1 year expiry date, and bookings can be made any time during that period.

Vouchers can also be ordered over the phone by credit or debit card and posted the next working day or you can call into to the salon to pick them up.

You can either buy a monetary value, or for a more personal touch, you can buy a specific treatment.

Monetary Vouchers can be spent on all treatments and products and don’t have to be used all at the same time, as long as it is within the 1 year expiry period.

Gift vouchers come gift wrapped in a envelope with one of our latest treatment menus.

Gift vouchers will be posted out the next working day with free delivery.


payment

We currently only accept cash payments.
