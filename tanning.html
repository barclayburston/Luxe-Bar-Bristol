Tanning

We’re extremely pleased to be offering the award winning Sienna X spray tans. Sienna X products contain natural active ingredients meaning they are paraben, alcohol and SLS free.




We welcome you into our warm spray tanning area and ensure you are fully prepared for your luxury tanning experience. Our tanning expert will discuss all aftercare with you to ensure the long lasting beautiful bronzed tanning experience.

We offer a full body tan or half a body tan, to suit every clients need.

Fake Tan Prep and Tips

Full body tanning takes 10 minutes spraying time and 10 minutes drying time. We recommend you leave your tan for 6-8 hours to develop before showering, or you can leave it on over night. Your spray tan will last 5-7 days depending on your skin care routine. Please see our “things to know” page for further aftercare advice.
